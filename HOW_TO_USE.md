## 🎓 For Candidates (Job Seekers)

### 1. Register & Login
1. Go to http://localhost:5174/register
2. Fill in your details
3. Select "Job Seeker"
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. Login at http://localhost:5174/login

### 2. Browse Jobs
1. Click "Browse Jobs" in the navbar
2. Use filters:
   - **Search** by keyword
   - **Job Type** (Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Internship, Freelance)
   - **Location** 
   - **Remote** (Remote only or On-site only)

### 3. View Job Details
1. Click on any job card
2. See full description, requirements, responsibilities
3. Check salary range and benefits
4. View required skills

### 4. Apply for Jobs
1. On the job detail page, scroll down
2. Fill in the "Apply for this Position" form:
   - Write a cover letter
   - Upload your resume (optional)
3. Click "Submit Application"
4. You'll be redirected to your applications page

### 5. Track Your Applications
1. Click "My Applications" in the navbar
2. See all your applications with status:
   - ⏳ **Pending** - Just submitted
   - 👀 **Reviewing** - Employer is reviewing
   - ⭐ **Shortlisted** - You made the shortlist!
   - 🎤 **Interview** - Interview scheduled
   - 🎉 **Offered** - Job offer received
   - ❌ **Rejected** - Not selected
3. View statistics at the top
4. Read employer notes if any

### 6. Dashboard
1. Click your username → Dashboard
2. See your application statistics
3. View recent applications
4. Quick access to all features

## 💼 For Employers

### 1. Register & Login
1. Go to http://localhost:5174/register
2. Fill in your details
3. Select "Employer"
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. Login at http://localhost:5174/login

### 2. Post a Job
1. Click "Post Job" in the navbar
2. Fill in the job details:
   - **Title** (e.g., "Senior Developer")
   - **Job Type** (Full-time, Part-time, etc.)
   - **Experience Level** (Entry, Mid, Senior, etc.)
   - **Location** and Remote option
   - **Salary Range** (optional)
   - **Description** - What the job is about
   - **Requirements** - What you're looking for
   - **Responsibilities** - What they'll do
   - **Skills Required** (comma-separated)
   - **Benefits** (optional)
   - **Application Deadline** (optional)
3. Click "Post Job"

### 3. View Applications
1. Click "Applications" in the navbar
2. See all applications for your jobs
3. Filter by specific job
4. View candidate details:
   - Name, email, phone, location
   - Years of experience
   - Skills
   - Cover letter
   - Resume (click to download)

### 4. Manage Applications
1. Click "Update Status" on any application
2. Choose a new status:
   - ⏳ **Pending** - Initial state
   - 👀 **Reviewing** - You're reviewing it
   - ⭐ **Shortlisted** - Candidate looks good
   - 🎤 **Interview** - Schedule an interview
   - 🎉 **Offered** - Make an offer
   - ❌ **Rejected** - Not a fit
3. Add notes (optional) - Internal notes about the candidate
4. Quick actions:
   - **Shortlist** - Quickly mark as shortlisted
   - **Reject** - Quickly reject

### 5. Dashboard
1. Click your username → Dashboard
2. See your posted jobs
3. View application counts per job
4. Quick access to post new jobs
